## Summary

- **Phase 1:** Flashcard Frenzy, Word Match  
- **Phase 2:** Word Flick, Speed Quiz Showdown (MVP then leaderboards), Word Chain (solo then multiplayer)  
- **Cross-cutting:** Dark mode and reduced motion from day one; 44px touch targets; quick sessions; consistent navigation and feedback.
